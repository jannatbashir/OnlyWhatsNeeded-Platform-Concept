Product Journey

How a product moves from community request to your hands. Nothing happens behind closed doors.

Ships to You
01
You asked for it
Over 4,200 people asked for a coffee flavour across Instagram comments and LinkedIn DMs. We tracked it, saw the pattern, and opened a vote.
02
Options vetted before you vote
Every option is reviewed by nutrition scientists and FSSAI-registered advisors first. You never vote on something that can't be made safely.
03
Community decides
67% of official votes chose coffee. We didn't pick the most marketable option. The verified majority spoke, and that became the R&D brief.
04
Prototype & blind taste test
50+ variations built. 120 OWNers blind-tested them. Version 2 won on taste, texture, and mixability. We built more to give you only what's needed.
05
7-parameter lab test every batch
Every batch tested before it ships: protein, amino acids, heavy metals, pesticides, microbial contamination. All results public.
06
Cost breakdown published
We show exactly what each pack costs: raw material, testing, packaging, logistics, margin. No hidden markups, no surprises.
